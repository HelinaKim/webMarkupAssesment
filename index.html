The highlights of Paramaribo

Paramaribo is the capital and the beating heart of Suriname. In the center you will find colored buildings in colonial style next to meters-high palm trees. Take a stroll through Palmengarten, the Surinamese answer to the traditional Dutch City Park. From the park, cross the Kleine Waterstraat and you're at the Surinaams Museum and Fort Zeelandia. Want to learn more about local history? Then join a guided tour of the fort. Then have dinner on the waterfront. Do you see yourself here already feasting on a portion of roti?
