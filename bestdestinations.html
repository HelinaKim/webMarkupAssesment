VACATION CURAÇAO

On a vacation on Curaçao, the white beaches lined with palm trees are present in large numbers. A beach vacation on the Caribbean Sea fulfills multiple wishes: from a dazzling sun vacation at Mambo Beach and Jan Thiel Bay to tranquility in West Point. Be sure to visit the colorful Handelskade during your city trip in Willemstad or make an excursion to the beautiful Christoffel Park. Bon bini on dushi Curaçao!?

VACATION SPAIN

A long coastline divided into several Costa's, temperamental cities like Barcelona and Madrid, the classic Andalucia; in southern Spain and delicious tapas: if you can experience "la buena vida" anywhere it is during a vacation in Spain. This country in southwestern Europe has been a favorite place for a flight or car vacation to Spain for years because of its location on the Mediterranean Sea.

VACATION GREECE

At TUI, you can take a vacation to Greece as you wish. The sun shines and the sea is always within reach in this southern European country. Choose one of the beautiful islands for this vacation. From Samos and Lesbos to Zakynthos, Greece is beautiful. But also the capital Athens is a real must-see with the Acropolis as one of the highlights.

VACATION KRETA

Party until you can't stand on your feet or visit as many cultural sights as possible. With a vacation in Crete you can do anything. And whether you settle down at the Venetian harbor of Rethymnon, meet Minosan snake goddesses in Iraklion or worship the sun in Hersonissos: from a hotel or apartment in Crete the sparkling Mediterranean is never far away.

VACATION ZAKYNTHOS

Say you vacation Zakynthos, then you say Shipwreck Beach. This world famous beach is the showpiece of the Greek island and we understand that. The shipwreck on the white sand and the azure water surrounded by white, steep rocks is a picture to see! Also be sure to meet the sea turtle during a vacation on Zakynthos. And the Verdia wines, not to mention.... Yamas!

VACATION ARUBA

Just looking for a vacation to Aruba will put a smile on your face! The pleasant tropical temperatures and white beaches with the unique divi divi trees form an idyllic setting for a sun vacation on the Caribbean Sea. Aruba is not called 'One Happy Island' for nothing. On a diving vacation on Aruba? Around the island you'll find interesting coral formations and the largest shipwreck in the Caribbean. Bon bini!
